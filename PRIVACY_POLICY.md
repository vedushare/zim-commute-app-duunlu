
# Privacy Policy for ZimCommute

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

ZimCommute ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Phone Number**: Used for authentication and account creation
- **Full Name**: For profile identification
- **Email Address**: Optional, for account recovery
- **Profile Photo**: Optional, for user identification
- **Home City**: To personalize ride recommendations
- **User Type**: Driver or Passenger designation

### Verification Information
- **ID Documents**: National ID or driver's license for identity verification
- **Vehicle Information**: For drivers - make, model, license plate, photos

### Location Data
- **Current Location**: When searching for rides (with your permission)
- **Ride Routes**: Origin and destination for ride matching

### Usage Data
- **Ride History**: Past rides, bookings, and ratings
- **App Usage**: Features used, screens visited, performance metrics
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version

### Payment Information
- **Wallet Balance**: In-app credit balance
- **Transaction History**: Credits purchased and spent

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services**: Match riders and drivers, facilitate bookings
- **Verify Identity**: Ensure safety through ID verification
- **Process Payments**: Manage wallet credits and transactions
- **Improve Experience**: Personalize recommendations and optimize performance
- **Communicate**: Send ride notifications, updates, and support messages
- **Ensure Safety**: Monitor for fraud, abuse, and safety violations
- **Comply with Law**: Meet legal obligations and respond to authorities

## Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

### Service Providers
- **Cloud Storage**: AWS/Google Cloud for data storage
- **Analytics**: Sentry for crash reporting and performance monitoring
- **Payment Processors**: For credit purchases (if applicable)

### Other Users
- **Drivers see**: Passenger name, photo, pickup location, rating
- **Passengers see**: Driver name, photo, vehicle info, rating

### Legal Requirements
- Law enforcement when required by law
- To protect rights, safety, or property
- In connection with legal proceedings

### Emergency Contacts
- Your designated emergency contacts receive ride details when you share a ride

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Authentication**: OTP-based phone verification
- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access to personal data
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request account and data deletion
- **Opt-Out**: Disable location services or notifications
- **Data Portability**: Export your data in a readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@zimcommute.co.zw

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Active Accounts**: While your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: 30 days after deletion (for recovery)
- **Legal Requirements**: Longer if required by law
- **Safety Records**: Incident reports retained for 7 years

## Children's Privacy

ZimCommute is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Offline Data

When offline, the app caches data locally on your device for functionality. This data is encrypted and synced when you reconnect.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
